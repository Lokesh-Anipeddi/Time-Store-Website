➣ Rolex:

Rolex is a Swiss watch manufacturer and designer based in Geneva. The company was founded in 1905 by Hans Wilsdorf and Alfred Davis in London. In 1908, they registered the brand name Rolex for their watches. In 1915, the company became Rolex Watch Co. Ltd.. Rolex is known for its quality and expertise. The company has submitted over 400 patents since its founding. In 1910, a Rolex watch became the first wristwatch to receive the Swiss Certificate of Chronometric Precision. Rolex is a leading name in luxury wristwatches. The company has 4,000 watchmakers in more than 100 countries. Rolex products are available in a range of budgets.
